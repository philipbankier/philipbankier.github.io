01 · Core Beliefs & Positions

What Yohei believes — extracted from public output.

Recurring positions across his X posts, project descriptions, talks, and interviews. Updated as new signals emerge.

🤖

Agents need scaffolding, not just smarter models

The model is only one layer. Agents need structured context, reusable tools, failure logging, and iterative improvement loops. "Failures should become durable improvements."

SOURCE: X post May 11 2026 · BabyAGI architecture · BabyAGI-2o design
🏗️

Build in public to learn faster

Shipping publicly is not vanity — it's a learning accelerator. "Build in public to learn faster, meet founders, and contribute to the ecosystem." This is his stated philosophy and lived practice.

SOURCE: yoheinakajima.com bio · TED AI talk · a16z presentation
🧠

Knowledge graphs are fundamental AI infrastructure

Four separate projects (Instagraph, Mindgraph, Prettygraph, babyblog) explore text-to-graph conversion. He treats knowledge graphs as the backbone for how agents should store and retrieve information.

SOURCE: GitHub repos instagraph, mindgraph, prettygraph · project descriptions
⚡

The simplest approach wins

Recurring language across project descriptions: "the simplest self-building coding agent" (Ditto), "the simplest self-building general autonomous agent" (BabyAGI-2o). He deliberately ships minimal implementations and iterates publicly.

SOURCE: GitHub descriptions for ditto, babyagi-2o · build-in-public pattern
🔌

Agents should self-extend their own capabilities

SelfMCP is a self-extending MCP server. BabyAGI-2o is a self-building agent. His trajectory is toward agents that create their own tools, skills, and extensions without human intervention.

SOURCE: selfMCP repo · babyagi-2o repo · X posts about agent autonomy
💼

VC firms should aggressively leverage AI

Untapped Capital uses AI-assisted startup research and a GPT-powered VC Associate. He presents globally on "How to run your VC firm using AI" including at a16z's GP-LP mixer. Ranked Top 20 thought leader in Data Driven VC Landscape report.

SOURCE: yoheinakajima.com · a16z SF Tech Week presentation · Data Driven VC report
