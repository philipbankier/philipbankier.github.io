Key Beliefs

The log IS the agent

Most agent frameworks bolt logging on last; ActiveGraph inverts this. The append-only event log is the source of truth. This yields deterministic replay, cheap forking, and end-to-end lineage — properties retrieval-and-summarization memory systems cannot provide.

Source: arXiv paper "The Log is the Agent" (May 2026)

Autonomy requires governance

"Trust is no longer a property of the model; it is a property of the system around it." The winners in agentic categories will not be the most autonomous systems, but the most governable ones.

Source: LinkedIn "The State of AI Going into 2026" (May 2026)

Data maturity is required for AI

"AI is forcing a data maturity upgrade. Humans once compensated for fragmentation. Agents cannot." Freshness is a performance attribute — stale data propagates errors at scale.

Source: LinkedIn article (May 2026)

The simplest approach wins

Recurring theme across projects: "Simplest self-building coding agent", "Simplest self-building general autonomous agent".
